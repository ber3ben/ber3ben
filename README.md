## hi! I'm bernie

I'm an information professional and digital content generalist. 

I specialize in data infrastructure, metadata systems, and the preservation of knowledge at the intersection of traditional library science and modern digital workflows with a major focus on structural data quality and ethical technology.

---

### Specialties

* **Metadata & Knowledge Systems:** Structuring, cleaning, and optimizing complex taxonomies and information hierarchies.
* **Responsible Automation:** Designing AI-assisted workflows that assist knowledge work without introducing bias or compromising data privacy.
* **Archival Preservation:** Exploring how digital history captures marginalized or niche subcultures, with a particular interest in queer archives.
* **Classification Ethics:** Auditing systems to understand how misclassification happens and how to prevent technical bias from obscuring truth.

---

### Toolkit

* **Data Engineering & Workflow Automation:** Python (Pandas, ETL pipelines), dbt, DuckDB, SQL, GitHub
* **Library Software Suites:** 10+ years of experience with the Springshare Suite (LibGuides, LibAnswers, etc.)
* **AI-Assisted Development:** Utilizing modern development environments (Cursor, Replit) for rapidly prototyping low-pressure, accessible user interfaces.

---

### Philosophy

I think digital tools should reduce cognitive load, not increase it. Whether constructing a production-grade data pipeline or designing an interface, I prioritize soft, user-friendly design patterns, clear categorization, and meticulous data integrity.
